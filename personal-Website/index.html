About Me:

Hi there. My name is Joseph, thanks for visiting my page.

I am a student at EWU finishing my degree in Computer Science. I really enjoy programming, there's tons to learn and I am driven to improve. I work as a Plus Tutor at the Eastern in the Computer Science department. I feel that teaching code is a great way to cement understanding of core topics. In my free time you'll find me at the gym, learning on Udemy or solving puzzles on leet code. I feel strongly that in order to be a good developer I need to always be improving. I have been lucky enough to get a great internship over the 2021 summer at Gramma Tech in Ithaca NY. This is a great opportunity for me to grow and learn.

Why Coding?

Programming and problem solving are inseparable and it's impossible to have one without the other. There's always a challenge that needs concurring or a problem that needs solving. This is why coding is so exciting, there is a never-ending number of challenges and just as many ways to complete them. From creating complex games to designing apps to make someone's life easier each task comes with its own individual solution. There is never only one way of doing something. Computer science has something for everyone, design, problem solving, critical thinking and creativity.
